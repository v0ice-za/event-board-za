Choosing Your Direction

Direction 1 — Full-Bleed
Maximum visual impact per event
Most Fever-like, premium feel
Images with dark tones look best
Fewer events visible above fold
Legibility depends on image quality
Direction 2 — Image Top
Text always readable (on surface bg)
Category tag adds context per card
Works well with any image palette
Less immersive than full-bleed
Slightly more chrome per card
Direction 3 — Compact Row
Most events visible at once
Fast to scan long lists
Works well if event quality is mixed
Least visual drama
Loses the image-led premium feel
Direction 4 — Hero + Grid
Editorial feel, highlights top event
More density below the hero
Good for "featured" event concept
Implies editorial curation (no v1 editor)
Grid cards are small on phone screens
