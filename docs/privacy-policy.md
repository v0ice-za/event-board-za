# Privacy Policy — Event Board ZA

**Effective date:** 27 June 2026
**Last updated:** 27 June 2026

Event Board ZA ("the App", "we", "us") is a free, ad-supported mobile app for
discovering events in Johannesburg, South Africa. The App is developed and
operated by **Voice Mijalkovic** (solo developer).

This policy explains what data is and is not collected when you use the App, who
processes it, and your rights under South Africa's **Protection of Personal
Information Act, 2013 (POPIA)**.

- **App name:** Event Board ZA
- **Developer / Responsible Party (POPIA):** Voice Mijalkovic
- **Contact for privacy queries:** **voji95@gmail.com**

If you have any question about this policy or wish to exercise any right
described below, email the address above.

## 2. Data the App itself collects

**None.** Event Board ZA does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal
information about you. Specifically, the App:

- has **no user accounts** — there is no sign-up, login, name, email, or password;
- does **not** ask for or record your **location**, contacts, photos, or device
  address book;
- has **no social features**, no messaging, no maps, no push notifications, and
  no in-app purchases;
- does **not** build a profile of you or sell any data.

Event data shown in the feed (event names, dates, venues, images, ticket links)
is public listing information sourced from event providers; it is not about you.

---

## 3. Data collected by third-party SDKs

The App uses a small number of third-party software development kits (SDKs) to
serve ads and to keep the App stable. These SDKs may collect limited device and
usage data **on their own behalf**, governed by their own privacy policies:

### 3.1 Google AdMob (advertising)

The App is free and supported by ads served through **Google AdMob**. To serve
and measure ads, AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as an advertising
ID), coarse device and network information, and ad-interaction data. This is used
to display ads and to prevent fraud and abuse.

- Google's policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- How Google uses data from apps that use its services:
  https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

### 3.2 Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics (Google)

The App uses **Firebase Analytics** to understand anonymous, aggregated usage
(for example, which screens are viewed and which event categories are browsed)
and **Firebase Crashlytics** to receive crash diagnostics so we can fix bugs.
These collect app-usage events and crash/diagnostic data associated with a
non-identifying app-instance identifier — not your name or email.

- Firebase privacy & security: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

We do **not** combine this SDK data with any identity, because the App holds no
identity to combine it with.

---

## 4. Why this data is processed

- **Ad serving (AdMob):** to keep the App free of charge.
- **Analytics (Firebase Analytics):** to understand which features are useful and
  improve the App.
- **Crash reporting (Crashlytics):** to detect and fix crashes and defects.

## 5. Your rights under POPIA

Even though the App collects no personal information itself, you have the
following rights in respect of any personal information processed about you under
POPIA:

- **Right of access** — to ask what personal information is held about you.
- **Right to correction** — to request correction of inaccurate information.
- **Right to objection / deletion** — to object to processing or request deletion
  where the law allows.
- **Right to complain** — to lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator.

To exercise the access, correction, or objection rights in relation to the App,
contact **voji95@gmail.com**. For SDK-collected data, you can also use the
controls Google provides (for example, resetting or limiting your device's
advertising identifier in your device settings).

**Information Regulator (South Africa):**
- Website: https://inforegulator.org.za
- Email: enquiries@inforegulator.org.za
